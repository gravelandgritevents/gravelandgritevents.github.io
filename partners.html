Partner value

Why partner with us

Trusted race-day presence

Your support appears in a setting where runners are paying attention: basecamp, race communications, event content, and the moments around the challenge itself.

Useful product discovery

Backyard Ultra runners care about kit, fuel, recovery, comfort, and practical support. Good products are noticed because they make the weekend better.

Local credibility

We are Bristol-based and community-led, with a route, atmosphere, and partner mix that make sense for local and like-minded brands.

Strong storytelling

Rain, mud, headtorches, crews, volunteers, and one more lap create natural photo, video, and social content without forcing the story.

Audience fit

Our runners and supporters are active, outdoors-minded, practical, resilient, and loyal to brands that show up in useful ways.

Flexible collaboration

We prefer partnerships that fit the brand and event properly, rather than fixed packages that look tidy but do not add much to race weekend.

Ways to work together

Flexible partnerships that add something useful

We are open to conversations with running, outdoor, wellness, food and drink, retail, media, and community-aligned brands. The best fit is simple: it should make the event better for runners, crews, volunteers, or supporters.

Product support

Fuel, drinks, recovery, kit, comfort, coffee, or other practical support that helps runners through the weekend.

Prizes and rewards

Thoughtful prizes for standout performances, community moments, runners-up, volunteers, or finish-area celebrations.

Retail and kit advice

Helpful pre-race advice, local store links, discount support, or race-weekend guidance that helps runners arrive prepared.

Basecamp activations

Simple, useful on-site presence for runners and crews, shaped around the event rhythm rather than a hard-sell stand.

Community support

Local partnerships that help make the weekend warmer, calmer, more inclusive, or easier for the people taking part.
