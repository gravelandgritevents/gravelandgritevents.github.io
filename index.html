What is a Backyard Ultra?

The Bristol Backyard Ultra is not a typical ultramarathon. Set within the historic parkland and woodland trails of Blaise Castle Estate, it’s a test of pacing, patience, and mental resolve.

No finish line, no target time, no fixed distance. Just a loop, a bell, and one question each hour: can you go again?

Runners complete a 6.7km loop every hour. Finish early and you rest. Finish late or miss the start? Race over.

The Course

The 6.7km loop winds through Blaise Castle Estate, combining woodland paths, open parkland, and gentle hills. Fully marked, easy to follow, with no navigation needed.
