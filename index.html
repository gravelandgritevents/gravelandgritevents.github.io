What is a Backyard Ultra?

The Bristol Backyard Ultra is a last-runner-standing endurance event. Runners complete a 4.167-mile (6.7km) loop every hour, on the hour. Finish the loop in time and you start again. Miss the bell, and your race is over.

There’s no set finish distance and no pressure to “go long”. Run one loop or run all day — this event is about pacing, persistence, and seeing how far you want to go.

The Course

The course is a professionally measured 4.167-mile loop set entirely within Blaise Castle Estate, one of central Bristol’s most beautiful wild spaces.

Expect a varied and runnable route featuring woodland trails, single track, open green fields, and gentle climbs — interesting enough to stay engaging lap after lap.

The loop starts and finishes at a central basecamp area with space for personal kit, crew support, and between-loop recovery.
