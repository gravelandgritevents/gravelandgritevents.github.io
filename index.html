What is a Backyard Ultra?

The Bristol Backyard Ultra is not a typical ultramarathon. It is a simple, relentless test of patience, pacing, and mental resolve, set within the historic parkland and woodland trails of Blaise Castle Estate in Bristol.

There is no finish line to chase, no target time, and no fixed distance. Just a loop, a bell, and one question asked again every hour: can you go again?

Runners complete a 4.167-mile (6.7 km) trail loop every hour, on the hour. You have 60 minutes to complete each loop. Finish early and you rest. Finish late, or fail to make the start line when the bell rings, and your race is over.

The event repeats hour after hour and continues until only one runner remains. That final runner must complete one last solo loop to be crowned Last One Standing.

Every other participant, regardless of distance covered, is recorded as a DNF. This is not a race about speed. It is about pacing, recovery, decision-making, and managing the mental challenge of repetition.

The Course

The course is 4.167 miles (6.7 km) long and winds through the varied trails of Blaise Castle Estate, combining woodland paths, babbling brooks, and open parkland.

Elevation varies between long flat sections, gentle climbs and descents, plus one killer hill on the route up to the castle. The loop is fully marked and easy to follow, so runners can focus solely on completing each lap and returning to the start before the next bell.

Bristol Backyard Ultra – Frequently Asked Questions

What is a Backyard Ultra?

A Backyard Ultra (also known as Last One Standing) is a unique endurance format. Runners complete a 6.7km (4.167-mile) loop every hour, on the hour. You must finish each loop within the hour to continue.

Is this Backyard Ultra suitable for beginners?

Yes — absolutely. While some runners aim to go deep into the event, many participants come with the goal of completing just a few loops. You can stop after any completed lap.

Is this an official Backyard Ultra event?

Yes. This event follows the official Backyard Ultra format: a 6.7km loop run every hour until one runner remains.

Where is the event held?

The event takes place at Blaise Castle Estate in Bristol, South West England.

What is the minimum age for entering?

As the event complies with UK Athletics rules for trail running, participants must be aged 20 or older on race day.
