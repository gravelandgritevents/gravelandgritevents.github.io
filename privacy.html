Website services

What we use and why

Email updates

Newsletter signups are handled by Mailchimp. If you subscribe, Mailchimp processes the email address you provide and manages unsubscribe links.

Event entry

Race entry links take you to Race Nation. Entries, payments, and participant details are handled by Race Nation under their own terms and privacy policy.

Maps and route files

The race information page uses Leaflet and OpenStreetMap tiles to show the course. You can also download the GPX route directly.

Gallery embeds

The gallery page embeds a Picflow gallery from Fir Tree Media so visitors can browse and download event photos.

Fonts and hosting

The site uses Google Fonts and is hosted as a static GitHub Pages website. These services may receive basic technical request information such as IP address and browser details.

Contact

For privacy questions, email info@gravelandgritevents.com.
